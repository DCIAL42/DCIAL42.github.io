About Me

Hello, my name is Daniel Cooke, I am a computer science student at DCU.
I started programming when I was around 12 years old and was very interested in it from the start, but I was not able to do much until I started the leaving cert.
Since then I have been programming both in school/college and in my spare time, trying out different programming languages, such as:

  • Python
  • C++
  • Javascript
  • Rust
  • Lua
as well as different libraries in these languages, such as, SDL in C++, and pygame and tkinter in python.

Over the past few years I have worked on both personal, group, and school programming projects, as well as making a few personal websites. Some of these projects include:
